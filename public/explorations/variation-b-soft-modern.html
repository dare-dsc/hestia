About this variation

Same copy, same door‑slide reveal — but the doors become two floating, fully‑rounded panels with a visible gutter, the image lives inside a soft bento frame instead of going edge‑to‑edge, and the accent shifts from clay to a muted sage. Onest’s rounded geometry plus Albert Sans reads calmer and more “considerate app” than editorial — closer to the feeling of a well‑made home‑services product than a magazine spread.
