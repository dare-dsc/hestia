About this variation

Same copy, same door‑slide reveal — but the palette drops to near‑black with a single desaturated ochre rule, the headline type goes to Archivo Black in all caps at poster scale, and the kitchen photography is pushed toward grayscale. Hard edges, no rounded corners, no soft shadows. Reads less “warm welcome” and more “a service that means business” — worth testing if the brand should feel calmer or more authoritative.
