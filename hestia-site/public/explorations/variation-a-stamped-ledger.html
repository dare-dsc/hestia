About this variation

Same copy, same door‑slide reveal as the live site — but the type goes heavier and unitalicised (Fraunces at black weight), the accent shifts to an inkier, more saturated red, and a rubber‑stamp motif plus dashed “perforation” edges on the doors lean into the “Seven checks, stamped before they start” idea from the very first frame. Reads more like a verified record than a warm invitation.
